👋 WELCOME Welcome to the official Volyo Solutions Pvt Ltd Discord server. This is our central hub for tracking daily work, sharing progress, coordinating across teams, and maintaining transparency within the organization. Please read this guide carefully before posting.

All team members are expected to post daily task updates in the designated channels. Consistent reporting keeps the team aligned and helps management track project progress effectively.

📂 CHANNEL STRUCTURE 📌 #read-me → Server rules, format guidelines & onboarding info 📋 #daily-tasks → Post your daily task log every working day ✅ #task-completed → EOD update — completed tasks, pending & notes 🚧 #blockers-issues → Report blockers or dependencies delaying your work 📢 #announcements → Company-wide updates (Admin/Manager only) 💬 #general → Team conversations & informal communication 🔒 #management-only → Restricted to managers and leadership only

📜 RULES ① Post your daily update every working day. Missing 3 consecutive updates without notice will be flagged to your manager. ② Use the correct channel for each message. Do not post task logs in #general. ③ Keep communication professional and respectful. No harassment or personal attacks. ④ Do not share confidential client data, source code, or internal documents in unsecured channels. ⑤ Tag your manager or use @here in #blockers-issues for time-sensitive issues. ⑥ Respond to manager pings within 2 working hours (9 AM – 6 PM IST). ⑦ Do not DM clients or external parties from this server. Use official email only.
